A Heritage Sojourn in the
Pilgrimage City

Bhubaneswar is characterised by its plethora of Hindu temples like Rajarani Temple, Lingaraja Temple, Brahmeshwar Temple, and more. People from all over the world come to pay their respects and seek the blessings of the deities worshipped here. To explore the depths of the city, one must visit during the winter months, between October and February, when the temperature remains pleasant.
